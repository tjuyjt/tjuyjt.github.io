文献阅读：

1.Investigation of crack initiation and propagation behavior of AISI 310 stainless steel up to very high cycle fatigue
The VHCF behavior of metallic materials is generally divided in two types, based on the crack initiation region . In first type, the difference in fatigue strength from 106 to 109 cycles is found more than 50 MPa, and the cracks are initiated from the internal defects of the material. A fish-eye region is obtained at the crack initiation and a stair-case type S–N curve is obtained.In second type, the cracks are initiated from the surface of the material due to the surface defects, heterogeneities, pores and absence of inclusions and microstructural defects . In this type of materials, the S–N curve between 106 and 109 cycles show horizontal asymptote without any transition at 107 cycles. The difference in fatigue strength from 106 to 109 cycles is found less than 50 MPa.
超高周疲劳行为可以根据起裂源区的不同分为两类，一类为内部起裂，一类为表面起裂。其中表面起裂的超高周疲劳行为的特点S-N曲线在107左右没有过渡区，106的疲劳强度与109的疲劳强度相差往往少于50MPa。
In this study, the VHCF behavior of Stainless Steel AISI 310 was investigated through the ultrasonic fatigue testing. In fatigue cycles, a horizontal asymptote of S–N curve was found without any step at 106 cycles. Surface crack initiation was found irrespective of the stress level. It was found that up to 106 cycles, the cracks initiated from carbide precipitates present on the grain boundaries. However, beyond 106 cycles, PSBs were developed at the surface of the specimen which acted as the fatigue crack initiation sites. Below a certain stress threshold, the PSBs were observed but declared as non-damaging. It was concluded that the PSBs were developed due to higher fatigue cycles instead of higher stresses.
一个换热管材料AISI310的超高周疲劳行为研究，其S-N曲线在106左右没有阶跃，起裂位置始终位于表面。106之前，裂纹起裂于晶界碳化物的沉积，106之后，试样起裂于驻留滑移带（PSBs）。在一定应力值一下，驻留滑移带可以发现，但是可以视为无损伤。驻留滑移带的产生是由于高循环圈数而不是高应力。
The surface of the specimens was polished to surface roughness 0.2 μm. Surface roughness of the material was determined with Atomic force microscope (AFM). Surface roughness Ra for different regions were measured and found up to 80 nm. Fig. 3b shows the 3D surface topography of the fatigue samples before testing. It can be seen that surface of the specimen were well polished and no such roughness on the samples was present.
将试样抛光至粗糙度为Ra0.2，并用原子力显微镜（AFM）进行了观测。

Fig. 4 shows the S–N curve for the material. The curve showed decreasing slope at higher stress levels, up to 106 cycles. This showed that at higher stress levels, the fatigue life was continuously decreasing. A semi-asymptote type curve was obtained and the curve became almost horizontal after 106 cycles.

In “Region 2”, the radial lines originated from the crack initiation point. The region was larger than the “Region 1”. Owing to these lines, the surface roughness was higher and leads to the conclusion that the crack propagation was slow and faced resistance in this region. The “Region 2” for the sample failed at 7.78 × 108 cycles was larger as compared to the specimen failed up to 1.63 × 106 cycles.

“Region 3” was the largest of all four regions and mostly occupied by the radial lines. The distance between the lines reached to 150 μm at some places, as shown in Fig. 6c. Cleavage fracture was observed with many secondary cracks and higher surface roughness as shown in Fig. 6d. This showed that the crack propagated very slowly here and faced highest resistance. Few lines were observed for the specimen failed at 7.78 × 108 cycles as compared to the 1.63 × 106 cycle’s specimen. This showed that at lower stress, the fatigue cycles were not creating any effect on the subsurface of the specimen. “Region 4” was unstable final fracture represented the fatigue overload. Ductile fracture with transgranular crack propagation was observed in this region.
此外，这篇文章的一种重点是用原子力显微镜对试样表面的PSBs进行了观测。文章的主要结论如下：
Fatigue behavior of AISI 310 was investigated up to very high cycles. S–N curve with horizontal asymptote was obtained without any step. It was found that the fatigue fracture can occur beyond 107 cycles in this material.

It was observed that all cracks were initiated from the surface of the specimen irrespective of the stress levels and the cycles before fatigue failure.

It was found that at higher stress levels, the specimen exhibited lower fatigue life and cracks initiated from carbide precipitates on the surface of specimen. However, above 106 cycles at lower stress levels, PSBs were found at the surface of the specimen and acted as the fatigue crack initiation site. The sharp edges of PSBs served as the fatigue crack initiation and propagation.

The horizontal S–N curve showed that little decrease in stress level increased the failure cycles significantly. The higher fatigue cycles were used in development of PSBs all over the surface of the specimen.

2.It was observed that all cracks were initiated from the surface of the specimen irrespective of the stress levels and the cycles before fatigue failure.
3.It was found that at higher stress levels, the specimen exhibited lower fatigue life and cracks initiated from carbide precipitates on the surface of specimen. However, above 106 cycles at lower stress levels, PSBs were found at the surface of the specimen and acted as the fatigue crack initiation site. The sharp edges of PSBs served as the fatigue crack initiation and propagation.

——Khan M K, Wang Q Y. Investigation of crack initiation and propagation behavior of AISI 310 stainless steel up to very high cycle fatigue[J]. International Journal of Fatigue, 2013, 54: 38-46.

文献阅读：

2.Numerical modelling of surface roughness effect on the fatigue behavior of Ti-6Al-4V obtained by additive manufacturing
It is usually difficult to quantify and understand the respective influence of these defects since in many studies, the early crack initiation is due to a combination of parameters including porosity, surface roughness, residual stresses and specific microstructures.
缺陷对高周疲劳寿命的影响很难量化，早期裂纹萌生可能与由孔隙率、表面粗糙度、残余应力和特定的微观结构等综合决定。
Most of existing models [13], [14], [15] accounting for the effect of the surface roughness on the HCF strength consider the surface as a series of micronotches. The theoretical stress concentration factor induced by the notch Kt=loc/nom is introduced, where loc is the stress at the notch tip and nom is the nominal stress. . Kt can then be calculated using analytical formulations [16] or FEM.
常规处理表面粗糙度的方法，采用一个Kt作为比值，通过解析公式或者有限元的方式进行计算，目前来说对于均匀和简单的形貌，这些常规方法可以取得一定的精度。
In the general case, the effect of stress gradient at the notch tip can have several physical consequences, either on the local stress state (the presence of strong gradients modifies the development of local microplasticity as compared to smooth specimens), and, under certain conditions, on the development of non- propagating cracks.
一般来说，缺口试样有两个影响， 首先是影响应力分布，其次是可能导致某些非扩展裂纹的产生。
Numerical simulations aims at fully capture the complexity of the local stress-strain fields induced by the surface roughness and to introduce them into an HCF strength prediction methodology.
数值模拟的目的是充分描述由于表面粗糙度引起的应力应变场的不均匀性，并将其引入高周疲劳强度的预测模型
Meshed volumes and surfaces are created either from surface micrography, surface scans (profilometry) or volume scans (tomography).
采用了三种建模的方式：微观摄影、表面扫描和断层分析。
In order to evaluate the effect of local plasticity on the near surface stress fields, two different constitutive models were considered: isotropic elasticity and elastoplastic behavior.
两种本构模型，各向同性弹性以及弹塑性。
In the case of cyclic plasticity, an Armstrong Frederic [32] model including two non linear kinematic hardenings and one isotropic hardening was chosen in order to fit experimental data.
弹塑性模型中引入了两个非线性运动硬化和一个各项同行硬化。
——Vayssette B, Saintier N, Brugger C, et al. Numerical modelling of surface roughness effect on the fatigue behavior of Ti-6Al-4V obtained by additive manufacturing[J]. International Journal of Fatigue, 2019, 123: 180-195.
