4.The role of microtexture on the faceted fracture morphology in Ti–6Al–4V subjected to high-cycle fatigue

It is commonly reported that fatigue crack initiation sites in titanium alloys are associated with the formation of near-basal plane faceting.Investigation of the fatigue crack initiation sites in specimens examined in the present study showed that they consisted of extended regions of faceted crack growth
钛合金的起裂源区与近基面的小平面的形成有关

Studies on the formation of such facets show that they preferentially form in grains oriented with a c-axis misorientation between 15° and 40° from the principal loading direction [5], [23]. Initiation is therefore favoured by grains with a moderately high critical resolved shear stress for basal slip coupled with a tensile component perpendicular to the basal plane.
起裂小平面与c轴的夹角

The planar nature of the facets coupled with the fact that they form on the basal plane can falsely suggest that their formation is the consequence of a brittle fracture. Although largely considered to form via a cleavage mechanism, there is evidence to suggest that facets are slip induced. Bridier et al. [5] observed slip markings on grains oriented for basal slip during fatigue loading using a scanning electron microscope (SEM). Additionally, Pilchak et al. [24] showed that the formation of a basal facet was the result of several load cycles with the crack progressively making its way across an α colony, demonstrating that facets in titanium are not formed via a brittle cleavage mechanism. In the present work the active deformation mechanisms within a faceted grain are examined using a transmission electron microscope (TEM).
作者认为小平面的形成不是准解理的脆性断裂，而是循环过程中滑移引起的。

这篇文章中大量详细讨论断口中的小平面的形成原因及影响，如果后期观测到自己的实验中也有这样的平面的话可以进行相应的学习和讨论。
——Bantounas I, Dye D, Lindley T C. The role of microtexture on the faceted fracture morphology in Ti–6Al–4V subjected to high-cycle fatigue[J]. Acta materialia, 2010, 58(11): 3908-3918.
